Chicken Cordon Bleu

Description

This Glazed Ham is a quick and easy dinner that everyone will love.

Just three basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Fully cooked ham steak
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook ham steak in skillet over medium heat until browned.

  2. Remove from skillet and drain fat.

  3. Melt butter in the same skillet over medium-low heat, stir in brown sugar.

  4. Return ham to the skillet.

    Cook, turning the ham often, until heated through and brown sugar has dissolved.

    Reduce heat if the butter/brown sguar starts to pop and splatter.
