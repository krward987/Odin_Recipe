Chicken Cordon Bleu

Description

This Chicken Cordon Bleu is a quick and easy dinner that everyone will love.

Just four basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Ham
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.

    Coat a 7x11in baking dish in non-stick spray.

  2. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4in thickness.

  3. Sprinkle each of side of chicken breast with salt.

    Place 1 slice of ham and 1 slice of Swiss cheese on top of the chicken breast.

  4. Roll up the chicken breast and secure with toothpicks.

  5. Place the rolled up chicken breast in the baking dish and sprinkle with bread crumbs.

  6. Bake until the chicken is no longer pink.

  7. Remove from the oven and place a slice of swiss cheese on top.

    Return to the over, remove when the cheese has melted.
